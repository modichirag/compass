Welcome to my homepage

I am final year Physics graduate student at University of California, Berkeley, affiliated with Berkeley Center for Cosmological Physics (BCCP).

My research is focussed on large-scale structures (LSS) of the Universe. This involves studying the distribution of matter on the scales of galaxies and larger to develop our understanding of the nature and evolution of our Universe, thus answering fundamental questions about big bang, dark matter, dark energy etc. To this end, I explore analytic, statistical and machine-learning methods to model LSS observations and develop inference techniques to maximize their science return.
